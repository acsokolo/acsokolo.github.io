Strategy turns the information into a clear plan. A web developer should: Create a sitemap, pages the website will include. Plan the site structure and the flow of the website. Decide on technologies HTML/CSS/JS, Choose hosting environment and domain. Figure out design direction and layout strategy and Establish what content will appear on each page.
